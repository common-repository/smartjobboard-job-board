== Description ==

Quickly and easily add a job board to your WordPress powered blog or website and start monetizing your traffic.
Job board can be embedded to any page of your website. You may manage your job board via easy to use Smartjobboard’s backend.

**Main Features and Benefits**

* Making money is easy!

  SmartJobBoard runs on auto-pilot, and you can set up multiple, customizable income streams to match your way of doing business.
  
* SEO optimized

  Your SmartJobBoard job pages are Google-compliant and fully Search Engine Optimized for fast, easy traffic generation.
  
* Intuitive admin interface

  Easily control and modify every detail of your job portal for the best performance. View quick stats, change your website design, set pricing options, configure job backfilling and much more.
  
* Performance Reports

  See which employers and jobs attract more traffic, get more applications, and market your job board accordingly

== Installation ==

1. Use the automatic installer or unzip and upload the folder sjb via FTP client to the wp-content/plugins directory
1. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in WordPress
1. Connect your Smartjobboard account to the plugin by inserting your Smartjobboard frontend URL into the settings field. The URL should be something like this: https://job-board-name.mysmartjobboard.com
1. Decide which page you want your job board to reside on from the dropdown
